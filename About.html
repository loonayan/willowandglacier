About Me

Hello! Thanks for stopping by

Hi, my name is Ayan, and I am passionate about tackling climate change.

As a youth member of the Sunrise organization, I am committed to spreading awareness about the urgent need for action.

That's why I started the Willow and Glacier website - to raise awareness about the critical issues we face and to share my concerns about the current state of the world.

The Willow Project, in particular, is something that I am deeply concerned about, and I want to use my platform to encourage others to take action and make a positive impact on our planet
